WELCOME

The Door Christian Fellowship Church is a Bible-based, Spirit-filled, Non-denominational Christian ministry with a passion to share the good news of Jesus Christ. Worship services follow a contemporary style in a casual environment where you'll hear relevant messages for everyday life. Our warm, friendly congregation has been serving the Nairobi area for more than 20 years.

HELPING YOUNG PEOPLE LIVE "EXTREME" LIVES FOR JESUS



Extreme Youth is a youth ministry that meets as a community committed to helping young people live "extreme" lives for Jesus, to bring the saving and transforming work of Jesus wherever our youth find themselves in our city.Our youth ministry is built on the conviction that we want to raise a generation of young men and women who's highest treasure in life is Jesus above all things, and who's greatest ambition in life will be to make Jesus known above every other pursuit.We want our young people to live with the conviction that:

"I have been crucified with Christ and I no longer live, but Christ lives in me.
The life I live in the body, I live by faith in the Son of God,
who loved me and gave himself for me."
Galatians 2:20


Extreme also runs many ministries, by the youth, for the youth. These run on a regular basis and are held at the church and around our community. Some of these include:

  • Bible Study

  • Sports

  • Focus Groups

  • Fellowship
